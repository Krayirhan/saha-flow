# İşAkış Logo Briefi

## Marka yönü

İşAkış; teknik servis ve saha ekiplerinin iş emirlerini, müşterilerini, ekiplerini ve operasyonlarını tek yerden yönetmesini sağlayan bir platformdur. Logo ilk bakışta düzen, görev takibi, ekip koordinasyonu ve tamamlanan iş fikrini vermelidir.

## Görsel üretim promptu

```text
Design a clean, memorable logo for a Turkish B2B SaaS platform called “İşAkış”.

İşAkış helps service companies and operational teams manage work orders, customers, teams and daily operations from one place. The logo must communicate workflow, organization, task tracking, coordination and completion clearly.

Create a simple geometric symbol that combines a connected workflow path, two or three coordinated nodes, and a subtle checkmark or completed task shape. The symbol should feel like work moving smoothly from creation to assignment to completion. Avoid literal location pins and avoid generic wrench or hammer imagery.

Use a modern, trustworthy and practical visual language. Preferred palette: deep navy blue, vivid teal and a small warm lime-green accent. Rounded corners, balanced proportions, strong silhouette, flat vector style, excellent readability at small sizes.

Create a standalone app icon and a horizontal wordmark with the exact Turkish name “İşAkış”. Use a clean sans-serif typeface with excellent support for Turkish characters. Transparent background, no mockup, no watermark, no extra text, no gradients, no 3D effects, no shadows.
```

## Negatif prompt

```text
No generic wrench, hammer, screwdriver, construction helmet, house icon, map pin, globe, complex illustration, tiny unreadable text, overly rounded cartoon style, gradient, 3D, photorealism, shadow, mockup or watermark.
```

## Kullanım varyantları

- Yatay logo: web sitesi ve dashboard başlığı
- Sembol: mobil uygulama ve favicon
- Tek renk versiyon: yazıcı, belge ve iş kıyafeti
- Koyu zemin ve açık zemin versiyonları
